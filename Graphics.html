Graphics Design

A graphic is a visual representation of an object. It could be a paper on the wall, a drawing on paper, or an image on the computer screen.

When it is displayed via the computer, it is mostly referred to as computer-generated graphics or graphics design. It is produced through hardware or a software program.

Graphic design can be part of the decor for a home when used as wall art, and it could also serve as a medium for marketing a brand. The latter will be the focus of this article.

Benefits of Graphics

Using it the right way will create a visual appeal, which is vital for audience engagement.

Some products need to be packaged to provide a means of protection, storage, and prepare them for distribution.

Types of Graphics

Visual identity is all about branding. A brand is a relationship between a business and its audienceWhile a brand identity explains the way an organization maintains this relationship. This is the basis of visual identity.

Advertising and marketingThese are all kinds of designs created for marketing and audience engagement purposes. Majority of graphic designers belong to this category.

Motivation Graphics Design These are just graphics in motion. Good examples are imagery, video, animation, and typography. Motion graphic is highly utilized in the media and gained popularity in recent years.
